📧 Email Settings (EmailJS)
📖 How to set up email sending (free):
  1. Go to emailjs.com and create a free account (200 emails/month free)
  2. Click Add New Service → choose Gmail, Outlook, or SMTP
  3. Go to Email Templates → Create New Template
  4. Use these variables in your template: {{to_email}}, {{to_name}}, {{from_name}}, {{invoice_no}}, {{invoice_date}}, {{due_date}}, {{amount}}, {{room_ref}}, {{check_in}}, {{check_out}}, {{hotel_address}}
  5. Copy your Service ID, Template ID, and Public Key below
⚠️ PDF attachment in email requires EmailJS paid plan. The invoice details are always included inline.
